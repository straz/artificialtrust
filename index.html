Artificial Trust

About this site

Artificial trust: the information diseases that affect machines, living things, and societies. In these essays, I attempt to explain how diseases can affect information in much the same way as organisms. As our natural intelligence has become augmented by artificial intelligence, we now see the rise of these new kinds of diseases that affect how we process and trust information that we rely on to survive.

Artificial Trust is a collection of related essays, offering a framework for understanding complex systems. It's an attempt to explain failure in the things we trust. ...read more
