An engine turns fuel into work.

Brains are engines which turn experiences into decisions.

{% include figure.html clickable="true" filename="brainengine3.svg" figureclass="col" imgclass="col" link="brainengine3.svg" %}

The brain learns from its own experiences. The brain can also learn from others who share their experiences.

The brain's decisions produce one of two kinds of outputs. Muscles perform some sort of action based on a decision. Also, the brain can make and store labels

Labels summarize the decision.
